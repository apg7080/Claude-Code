# Sous vide chicken breasts (uses your immersion circulator)

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Yield**: 3 breasts (~1.5 lb)

## Method

Season 3 breasts with olive oil, garlic, lemon, oregano. Sous vide 150°F / 1.5 hr. Ice-bath, refrigerate sealed up to 5 days. Sear 1 min/side before serving.
