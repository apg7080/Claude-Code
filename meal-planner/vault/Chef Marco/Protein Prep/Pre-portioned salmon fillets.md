# Pre-portioned salmon fillets

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Yield**: 4 portions

## Method

Portion 1.5 lb salmon into 6 oz pieces. Pat dry, vacuum-seal or wrap. Refrigerate up to 2 days, freeze beyond. Cook same-day for best texture.
