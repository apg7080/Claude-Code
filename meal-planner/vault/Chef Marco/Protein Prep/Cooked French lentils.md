# Cooked French lentils

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Yield**: ~4 cups cooked

## Method

Simmer 1.5 cups dry lentils with bay leaf and garlic for 22 min. Drain, cool. Refrigerate 5 days.
