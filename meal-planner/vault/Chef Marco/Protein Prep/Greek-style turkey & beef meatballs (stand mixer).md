# Greek-style turkey & beef meatballs (stand mixer)

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Yield**: ~30 meatballs

## Method

Use stand mixer with paddle to combine 1 lb turkey + 1 lb beef + grated onion, garlic, parsley, oregano, egg, almond flour. Bake at 400°F / 18 min on parchment. Cool, refrigerate 4 days or freeze.
