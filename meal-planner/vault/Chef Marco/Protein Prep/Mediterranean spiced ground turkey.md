# Mediterranean spiced ground turkey

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Yield**: ~1.25 lb cooked

## Method

Brown 1.5 lb ground turkey with onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, smoked paprika. Cool, refrigerate 4 days.
