# Roasted boneless chicken thighs

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Yield**: ~2 lb cooked

## Method

Toss 2 lb boneless skinless thighs with olive oil, oregano, garlic, lemon zest. Roast 425°F / 22 min. Cool, slice or shred. Refrigerate 4 days.
