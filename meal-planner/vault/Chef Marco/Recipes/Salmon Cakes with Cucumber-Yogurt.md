# Salmon Cakes with Cucumber-Yogurt

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 25 min · **Active**: 20 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: stovetop, stand_mixer

## Ingredients

- 1 lb salmon (or 2 cans good-quality)
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup almond flour
- Dill, lemon zest, scallion
- Plain Greek yogurt + cucumber + dill (sauce)

## Steps

1. Use stand mixer paddle: combine salmon, egg, almond flour, herbs (reserve unsalted portion for baby).
2. Form into 6 small adult cakes + 2 mini baby cakes.
3. Pan-fry in olive oil 3 min/side.
4. Adults: yogurt-cucumber sauce. Baby: plain cake fingers + plain yogurt.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Salmon cakes hold together as soft fingers — ideal BLW. Make 2 baby-sized patties without salt; cook in same pan.
