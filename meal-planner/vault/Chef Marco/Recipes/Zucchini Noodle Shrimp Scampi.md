# Zucchini Noodle Shrimp Scampi

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 25 min · **Active**: 25 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: stovetop

## Ingredients

- 1 lb shrimp
- 3 zucchini, spiralized
- 4 garlic cloves
- White wine (adults), butter, lemon, parsley

## Steps

1. Sauté garlic in butter, add shrimp 3 min.
2. Deglaze with wine (adults' pan). Add zoodles, cook 2 min.
3. Lemon and parsley. Baby: short zoodle pieces + minced shrimp + a little olive oil.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Zoodles can be too long/slippery for baby — chop into short pieces. Finely mince shrimp.
