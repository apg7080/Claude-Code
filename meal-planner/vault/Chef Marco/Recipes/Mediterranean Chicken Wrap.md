# Mediterranean Chicken Wrap

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 10 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: none

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Sous vide chicken breasts (uses your immersion circulator)]]

## Ingredients

- Sliced sous vide chicken
- Whole-wheat tortilla
- Hummus, cucumber, tomato, feta (adults)

## Steps

1. Spread hummus on tortilla.
2. Layer chicken + veg + feta. Roll.
3. Baby: chicken strips + tortilla pieces + soft cucumber.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Pull a few pieces of chicken aside for baby. Tortilla strips can be eaten plain.
