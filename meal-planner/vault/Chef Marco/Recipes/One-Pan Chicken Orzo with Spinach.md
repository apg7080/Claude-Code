# One-Pan Chicken Orzo with Spinach

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Roasted boneless chicken thighs]]

## Ingredients

- Prepped chicken thighs, shredded
- 1 cup orzo
- 2.5 cups low-sodium broth
- 3 cups spinach
- Lemon, parm (adults)

## Steps

1. Toast orzo in oil, 3 min.
2. Add broth, simmer 10 min.
3. Stir in chicken and spinach, 3 min more.
4. Adults: lemon + parm. Baby: small portion plain.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Soft orzo + chicken shreds are perfect for self-feeding (some scoop, some pinch).
