# Mediterranean Lentil & Roasted Veg Bowl

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 35 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: oven

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Cooked French lentils]]

## Ingredients

- 2 cups prepped lentils
- 1 sweet potato, cubed
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- Tahini-lemon sauce (adults)
- Olive oil, cumin, paprika

## Steps

1. Roast veg at 425°F / 22 min.
2. Warm lentils with olive oil + cumin.
3. Bowls: lentils, roasted veg. Adults: tahini sauce.
4. Baby: mash sweet potato into lentils for spoon-feeding texture.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Lentils + soft roasted veg are baby gold. Mash slightly. Skip tahini drizzle if tahini hasn't been introduced.
