# Greek Chicken Lunch Bowl (uses prep)

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 10 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: none

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Roasted boneless chicken thighs]]

## Ingredients

- Prepped chicken thighs
- Cucumber, tomato, hummus
- Pita (adults)

## Steps

1. Plate chicken + veg + hummus.
2. Adults: pita. Baby: chicken pieces + soft cucumber sticks.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Easy assembly — chicken pieces, soft cucumber, halved cherry tomato.
