# Sheet Pan Greek Meatballs with Veggies

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 35 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Greek-style turkey & beef meatballs (stand mixer)]]

## Ingredients

- 12 prepped meatballs
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 1 red onion, wedges
- Olive oil, oregano
- Tzatziki (adults)

## Steps

1. Toss veggies with oil and oregano. Roast 425°F / 18 min.
2. Add meatballs to warm through, 8 more min.
3. Adults: tzatziki and feta. Baby: meatballs cut in strips + soft pepper/zucchini.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Cut meatballs in quarters for baby (length-wise strips, not rounds, to reduce choking risk). Roasted soft veggies are excellent BLW.
