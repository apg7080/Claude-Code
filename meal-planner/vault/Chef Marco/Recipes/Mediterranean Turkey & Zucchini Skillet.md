# Mediterranean Turkey & Zucchini Skillet

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 25 min · **Active**: 20 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Mediterranean spiced ground turkey]]

## Ingredients

- 1.25 lb prepped Mediterranean turkey
- 2 zucchini, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives (adults only)
- Crumbled feta (adults)
- Lemon, olive oil

## Steps

1. Sauté zucchini in olive oil 6 min until tender.
2. Add tomatoes, cook 3 min until they burst.
3. Stir in prepped turkey to warm through, 3 min.
4. Adults: top with olives, feta, lemon. Baby: serve a portion of soft zucchini + plain turkey crumbles.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Soft zucchini sticks are excellent for self-feeding. Use unseasoned portion of turkey if possible (set aside before adding spices).
