# Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Apples & Fennel

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 45 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven, stovetop

## Ingredients

- 1 pork tenderloin (~1.25 lb)
- 2 apples, wedges
- 1 fennel bulb, wedges
- Olive oil, garlic, rosemary (adults), Dijon

## Steps

1. Sear tenderloin all sides in oven-safe skillet, 4 min.
2. Add apples + fennel + Dijon. Roast at 400°F for 18 min until 145°F internal.
3. Rest 5 min, slice thin. Baby: small pieces of pork + soft apple + soft fennel.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Pork tenderloin is tender and easy to chew when sliced thin. Roasted apples become very soft — perfect for baby. Skip rosemary in baby's portion.
