# Sous Vide Cod with Lemon-Herb Butter

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 40 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: sous_vide, stovetop

## Ingredients

- 3 cod fillets
- Butter, lemon, dill, parsley
- Asparagus, olive oil

## Steps

1. Sous vide cod with herb butter at 130°F / 30 min.
2. Roast asparagus at 425°F / 10 min.
3. Plate. Baby: flaked cod + soft asparagus tips.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Sous vide cod is silky-soft — great for baby. Check carefully for bones.
