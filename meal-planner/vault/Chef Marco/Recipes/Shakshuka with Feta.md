# Shakshuka with Feta

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: stovetop

## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp cumin (adults)
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Feta, parsley (adults)
- Bread for adults / soft toast strips for baby

## Steps

1. Sauté onion + garlic in oil, 5 min.
2. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer 10 min.
3. Make wells, crack eggs in, cover and cook 6-8 min until yolks set.
4. Adults: feta and bread. Baby: scoop soft tomato + chopped egg, toast strips.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Soft eggs and tomato are great. Make sure egg yolk is set fully for baby. Skip cumin and salt in baby's portion if sensitive — scoop baby's portion before adding spices.
