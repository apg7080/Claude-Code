# Greek Yogurt Bowl with Berries

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 5 min · **Active**: 5 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: none

## Ingredients

- Greek yogurt (full-fat for baby, 0% or 2% adults)
- Mixed berries
- Chia seeds, honey (ADULTS only — never for baby under 12 mo)
- Almond butter (adults)

## Steps

1. Adults: yogurt + berries + chia + honey + nut butter.
2. Baby: plain yogurt + smashed berries (no honey, no nuts).

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Plain whole-milk Greek yogurt for baby — NO honey under 12 months. Berries should be quartered/squished.
