# Spinach & Feta Frittata

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven, stovetop

## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 5 oz baby spinach
- 1/2 cup feta (adults' side)
- 1/4 cup milk
- Onion, garlic, olive oil

## Steps

1. Sauté onion, garlic, spinach until wilted.
2. Whisk eggs + milk, pour over.
3. Crumble feta over 3/4 of the pan (leave baby's quadrant plain).
4. Bake at 375°F / 15 min until set. Slice into fingers for baby.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Frittata wedges are perfect BLW shape. Use less feta in baby's quadrant (or skip).
