# Sous Vide Lemon-Herb Chicken Plate

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 15 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: sous_vide, stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Sous vide chicken breasts (uses your immersion circulator)]]

## Ingredients

- 1 prepped sous vide chicken breast
- 1 head broccoli florets
- 1 cup couscous (or skip for low-carb)
- Lemon, butter, parsley

## Steps

1. Quick-sear sous vide chicken 1 min/side for color.
2. Steam broccoli 5 min until tender.
3. Cook couscous per package (3 min).
4. Slice chicken. Adults: lemon-butter pan sauce. Baby: shredded chicken + soft broccoli florets.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Sous vide chicken stays incredibly tender — ideal for baby. Slice thin or shred. Skip the pan sauce salt for baby.
