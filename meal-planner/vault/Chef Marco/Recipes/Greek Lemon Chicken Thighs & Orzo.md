# Greek Lemon Chicken Thighs & Orzo

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 45 min · **Active**: 20 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: oven, stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Roasted boneless chicken thighs]]

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lb boneless chicken thighs (or use prepped)
- 1 cup orzo
- 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon (juice + zest)
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 cup baby spinach
- Crumbled feta to finish (adults)

## Steps

1. If not prepped: toss thighs with oil, garlic, oregano, lemon zest. Roast at 425°F for 22 min.
2. Toast orzo in 1 tbsp oil until golden (3 min). Add broth, simmer 10 min.
3. Stir spinach into orzo until wilted. Squeeze lemon juice in.
4. Plate orzo, slice chicken on top. Adults: feta + extra lemon. Baby: chop small.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Shred chicken finely; serve baby plain orzo tossed with a little broth (no added salt). Skip lemon zest on baby's portion.
