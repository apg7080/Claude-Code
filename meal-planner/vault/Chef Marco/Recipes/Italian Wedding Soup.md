# Italian Wedding Soup

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Greek-style turkey & beef meatballs (stand mixer)]]

## Ingredients

- 12 prepped meatballs
- 6 cups low-sodium broth
- 1/2 cup acini di pepe pasta
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 1 carrot, diced
- Parmesan (adults)

## Steps

1. Simmer broth + carrot, 6 min.
2. Add pasta, cook 8 min.
3. Add meatballs and spinach, 3 more min.
4. Adults: Parm. Baby: cool, serve in shallow bowl.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Use small pasta (acini di pepe). Cut meatballs into strips for baby. Reserve baby's portion before final salt.
