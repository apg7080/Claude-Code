# Mediterranean Tuna Salad

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 10 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: none

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Hard-boiled eggs]]

## Ingredients

- 2 cans tuna in olive oil
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Lemon, capers, parsley, olive oil
- Lettuce wraps or whole-grain bread

## Steps

1. Mix tuna + chopped eggs + lemon + capers + olive oil.
2. Adults: lettuce wraps or sandwich.
3. Baby: separate plate — mashed egg + avocado + tiny bit plain tuna.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> For baby: plain mashed avocado + chopped hard-boiled egg + a tiny amount of plain tuna (limit baby's tuna due to mercury — once a week max).
