# Cottage Cheese & Tomato Bowl

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 5 min · **Active**: 5 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: none

## Ingredients

- Cottage cheese (low-sodium for baby)
- Cherry tomatoes, cucumber
- Olive oil, black pepper, dill

## Steps

1. Spoon cottage cheese into bowls, top with chopped veg and oil.
2. Baby: shallow bowl with low-sodium cottage cheese + soft cucumber sticks.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Use low-sodium cottage cheese for baby (or rinse briefly). Soft tomato + cucumber are great.
