# Chicken Souvlaki Bowls

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 20 min · **Active**: 20 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Roasted boneless chicken thighs]]

## Ingredients

- Prepped chicken thighs, sliced
- 1 cucumber
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 red onion, thin sliced
- Greek yogurt + dill + garlic = tzatziki (adults)
- Plain Greek yogurt for baby

## Steps

1. Warm sliced chicken in a skillet, 3 min.
2. Build bowls: chicken, veg, tzatziki for adults.
3. Baby: chopped chicken, soft cucumber sticks, plain yogurt to dip.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Cucumber sticks (peeled) and soft tomato are great BLW. Plain yogurt (no honey) for baby instead of tzatziki.
