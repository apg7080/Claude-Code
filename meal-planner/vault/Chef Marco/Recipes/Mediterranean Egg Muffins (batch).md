# Mediterranean Egg Muffins (batch)

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: makes 12 (2 adults + baby for ~3 days)
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven

## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- Spinach, sun-dried tomato, scallion
- Feta (adults' batch)
- Olive oil

## Steps

1. Whisk eggs + veg. Divide into greased muffin tin.
2. Add feta to 8 cups (adult). Leave 4 cups plain (baby).
3. Bake at 350°F / 18 min. Refrigerate up to 4 days.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Tear muffin into pinch-sized pieces. Reserve 4 muffins as 'baby version' with no salt and less feta.
