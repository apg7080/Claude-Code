# Mediterranean Power Smoothie

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 5 min · **Active**: 5 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby (small portion)
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: immersion_blender

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 banana
- 1 cup berries
- 1 cup spinach
- 1 tbsp almond butter (adults)
- Milk to thin

## Steps

1. Blend everything with immersion blender.
2. Pour for adults. Baby: a small portion in an open cup with assistance.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Best in an open cup with help, or as part of a meal. Avoid adding honey. Smoothies aren't ideal as a primary BLW item — pair with finger foods.
