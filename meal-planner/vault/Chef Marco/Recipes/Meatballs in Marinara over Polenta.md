# Meatballs in Marinara over Polenta

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 25 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Greek-style turkey & beef meatballs (stand mixer)]]

## Ingredients

- 12 prepped meatballs
- 2 cups marinara
- 1 cup polenta + 4 cups water/milk
- Parmesan (adults)

## Steps

1. Whisk polenta into boiling liquid, simmer 12 min stirring.
2. Warm meatballs in marinara, 8 min.
3. Plate polenta, meatballs on top. Adults: Parm. Baby: small portion meatball strips + soft polenta.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Soft polenta is excellent for spoon practice. Cut meatballs in strips lengthwise for baby.
