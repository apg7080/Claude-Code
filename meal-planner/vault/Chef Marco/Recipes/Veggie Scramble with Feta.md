# Veggie Scramble with Feta

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 10 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: stovetop

## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- Spinach, tomato, scallion
- Feta (adults)
- Olive oil

## Steps

1. Sauté veg 3 min.
2. Add whisked eggs, scramble soft.
3. Adults: feta on top. Baby: small pieces, no feta.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Soft scrambled eggs are excellent BLW. Cut into pinch-sized pieces. Skip feta for baby's portion.
