# Garlic Shrimp & Cauliflower Rice

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 20 min · **Active**: 20 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: stovetop

## Ingredients

- 1 lb shrimp, peeled
- 12 oz cauliflower rice
- 4 garlic cloves
- Olive oil, lemon, parsley
- Red pepper flakes (adults)

## Steps

1. Sauté garlic in oil, 1 min. Add shrimp, cook 3 min until pink.
2. Sauté cauli rice in same pan with more garlic, 6 min.
3. Combine, finish with lemon and parsley. Adults: pepper flakes.
4. Baby: very finely chopped shrimp + soft cauli rice + steamed peas.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Chop shrimp very fine for baby (or skip — shrimp introduction varies; check pediatrician). Soft cauli rice is great. Avoid red pepper flakes in baby's portion.
