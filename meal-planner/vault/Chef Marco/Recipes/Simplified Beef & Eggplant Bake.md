# Simplified Beef & Eggplant Bake

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 55 min · **Active**: 25 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven, stovetop

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 large eggplant, sliced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- Onion, garlic, oregano, cinnamon (pinch)
- 1/2 cup feta (adults)

## Steps

1. Roast eggplant slices at 425°F / 20 min.
2. Brown beef + onion + garlic. Add tomatoes + spices, simmer 10 min.
3. Layer eggplant + sauce in baking dish, top with feta on adults' side.
4. Bake 15 min. Baby: scoop a portion before topping with feta.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Roasted eggplant is very soft and mashable. Beef sauce is ideal — reserve baby's portion before adding wine/salt.
