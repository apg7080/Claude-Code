# Pesto Salmon Bowls with Cauliflower Rice

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven, stovetop

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Pre-portioned salmon fillets]]

## Ingredients

- 2 salmon portions (6 oz each)
- 12 oz cauliflower rice
- 3 tbsp basil pesto (adults)
- 1 avocado
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Olive oil, lemon

## Steps

1. Roast salmon at 400°F for 12 min, skin-side down.
2. Sauté cauli rice in olive oil with garlic, 6 min.
3. Bowls: cauli rice base, salmon on top, tomato, avocado. Adults: pesto drizzle.
4. Baby: flake plain salmon, soft cauli rice, mashed avocado.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Flake salmon thoroughly checking for bones. Skip pesto for baby (high salt) — serve plain salmon flakes + soft cauli rice + a few avocado strips.
