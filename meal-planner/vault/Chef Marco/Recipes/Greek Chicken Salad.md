# Greek Chicken Salad

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 15 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: none

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Roasted boneless chicken thighs]]

## Ingredients

- Prepped chicken thighs, chopped
- Cucumber, tomato, red onion, olives
- Feta, olive oil, lemon, oregano

## Steps

1. Combine chopped veg with chicken.
2. Adults: dressing + olives + feta.
3. Baby: a few pieces chicken, soft cucumber sticks, halved cherry tomato (quartered if grape size).

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Skip raw onion and olives for baby. Soft cucumber sticks + tomato + chicken pieces work well.
