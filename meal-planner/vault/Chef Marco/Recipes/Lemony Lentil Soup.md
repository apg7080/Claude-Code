# Lemony Lentil Soup

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 35 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: stovetop, immersion_blender

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Cooked French lentils]]

## Ingredients

- 3 cups prepped lentils
- 1 onion, carrot, celery (mirepoix)
- 4 cups low-sodium broth
- 1 lemon
- 2 cups spinach
- Olive oil, cumin, garlic

## Steps

1. Sauté mirepoix 6 min. Add garlic, cumin, 1 min.
2. Add lentils + broth, simmer 10 min.
3. Stir in spinach. Use immersion blender to partially purée (leave texture).
4. Adults: lemon, olive oil drizzle. Baby: cool, serve in shallow bowl with pre-loaded spoon.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Use immersion blender to partially purée for a baby-safe texture. Reserve baby's portion BEFORE adding salt/lemon (citrus is fine but can be acidic).
