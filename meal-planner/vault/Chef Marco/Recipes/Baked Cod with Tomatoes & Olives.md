# Baked Cod with Tomatoes & Olives

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 30 min · **Active**: 10 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven

## Ingredients

- 2 cod fillets (~6 oz each) + 1 small fillet for baby
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes
- 1/3 cup kalamata olives (adults)
- 3 garlic cloves, sliced
- Olive oil, oregano, lemon

## Steps

1. Toss tomatoes with oil, garlic, oregano. Roast at 425°F / 10 min.
2. Nestle cod into pan, return to oven 12 min until flaky.
3. Adults: scatter olives, lemon juice. Baby: flake plain cod with soft burst tomatoes (skin removed).

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Cod is mild and flaky — perfect for BLW. Reserve baby's portion before adding olives. Check for bones carefully.
