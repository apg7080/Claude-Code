# Tuscan White Bean & Sausage Stew

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 35 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein · **Tools**: stovetop

## Ingredients

- 12 oz Italian chicken sausage (adults)
- 2 cans cannellini beans, drained
- Kale, onion, garlic, carrot
- 4 cups low-sodium broth
- Parm rind (adults)

## Steps

1. Sauté onion, carrot, garlic 6 min.
2. Add beans + broth, simmer 10 min. RESERVE baby's portion now.
3. Add sliced sausage and kale, simmer 10 more min for adults.
4. Baby: bean and veg portion, mashed.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Reserve a portion of beans + veggies BEFORE adding sausage (sausage is high-sodium for baby). Mash beans slightly.
