# Mediterranean Turkey Stuffed Peppers

Up: [[Chef Marco - Meal Plan]]

**Total**: 50 min · **Active**: 15 min · **Serves**: 2 adults + baby
**Diet**: mediterranean, high-protein, low-carb · **Tools**: oven

**Uses prepped**: [[Protein Prep/Mediterranean spiced ground turkey]]

## Ingredients

- 1.25 lb prepped turkey
- 4 bell peppers, halved
- 1/2 cup cooked rice
- 1/2 cup feta (adults)
- 1 cup marinara
- Fresh parsley

## Steps

1. Mix turkey + rice + half the marinara.
2. Fill peppers, top with remaining marinara.
3. Bake at 400°F covered 25 min, uncover 10 min.
4. Adults: feta on top. Baby: scoop filling only, mash slightly.

## 👶 Baby (10 mo) notes

> [!warning] Baby modifications
> Scoop filling for baby — skip the pepper shell (tougher skin can be tricky). Filling is soft and flavorful.
