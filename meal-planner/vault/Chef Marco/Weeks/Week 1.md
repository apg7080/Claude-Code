## Shopping list

### Pesto Salmon Bowls with Cauliflower Rice
- [ ] 2 salmon portions (6 oz each)
- [ ] 12 oz cauliflower rice
- [ ] 3 tbsp basil pesto (adults)
- [ ] 1 avocado
- [ ] Cherry tomatoes, halved
- [ ] Olive oil, lemon

### Greek Lemon Chicken Thighs & Orzo
- [ ] 1.5 lb boneless chicken thighs (or use prepped)
- [ ] 1 cup orzo
- [ ] 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- [ ] 3 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 lemon (juice + zest)
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1 tsp dried oregano
- [ ] 1 cup baby spinach
- [ ] Crumbled feta to finish (adults)

### Mediterranean Turkey & Zucchini Skillet
- [ ] 1.25 lb prepped Mediterranean turkey
- [ ] 2 zucchini, diced
- [ ] 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- [ ] 1/2 cup kalamata olives (adults only)
- [ ] Crumbled feta (adults)
- [ ] Lemon, olive oil

### Chicken Souvlaki Bowls
- [ ] Prepped chicken thighs, sliced
- [ ] 1 cucumber
- [ ] 1/2 red onion, thin sliced
- [ ] Greek yogurt + dill + garlic = tzatziki (adults)
- [ ] Plain Greek yogurt for baby

### Shakshuka with Feta
- [ ] 5 eggs
- [ ] 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- [ ] 1 onion, diced
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves
- [ ] 1 tsp cumin (adults)
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] Feta, parsley (adults)
- [ ] Bread for adults / soft toast strips for baby

### Baked Cod with Tomatoes & Olives
- [ ] 2 cod fillets (~6 oz each) + 1 small fillet for baby
- [ ] 1 pint cherry tomatoes
- [ ] 1/3 cup kalamata olives (adults)
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves, sliced
- [ ] Olive oil, oregano, lemon

### Garlic Shrimp & Cauliflower Rice
- [ ] 1 lb shrimp, peeled
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves
- [ ] Olive oil, lemon, parsley
- [ ] Red pepper flakes (adults)

### Greek Chicken Lunch Bowl (uses prep)
- [ ] Prepped chicken thighs
- [ ] Cucumber, tomato, hummus
- [ ] Pita (adults)

### Mediterranean Tuna Salad
- [ ] 2 cans tuna in olive oil
- [ ] Hard-boiled eggs
- [ ] Lemon, capers, parsley, olive oil
- [ ] Lettuce wraps or whole-grain bread

### Mediterranean Chicken Wrap
- [ ] Sliced sous vide chicken
- [ ] Whole-wheat tortilla
- [ ] Hummus, cucumber, tomato, feta (adults)

### Greek Yogurt Bowl with Berries
- [ ] Greek yogurt (full-fat for baby, 0% or 2% adults)
- [ ] Mixed berries
- [ ] Chia seeds, honey (ADULTS only — never for baby under 12 mo)
- [ ] Almond butter (adults)

### Veggie Scramble with Feta
- [ ] Spinach, tomato, scallion
- [ ] Feta (adults)
- [ ] Olive oil

### Mediterranean Egg Muffins (batch)
- [ ] 8 eggs
- [ ] Spinach, sun-dried tomato, scallion
- [ ] Feta (adults' batch)

### Mediterranean Power Smoothie
- [ ] 1 cup Greek yogurt
- [ ] 1 banana
- [ ] 1 cup berries
- [ ] 1 cup spinach
- [ ] 1 tbsp almond butter (adults)
- [ ] Milk to thin

### Cottage Cheese & Tomato Bowl
- [ ] Cottage cheese (low-sodium for baby)
- [ ] Cherry tomatoes, cucumber
- [ ] Olive oil, black pepper, dill
