## Shopping list

### Simplified Beef & Eggplant Bake
- [ ] 1 lb ground beef
- [ ] 1 large eggplant, sliced
- [ ] 1 can crushed tomatoes
- [ ] Onion, garlic, oregano, cinnamon (pinch)
- [ ] 1/2 cup feta (adults)

### Sous Vide Lemon-Herb Chicken Plate
- [ ] 1 prepped sous vide chicken breast
- [ ] 1 head broccoli florets
- [ ] 1 cup couscous (or skip for low-carb)
- [ ] Lemon, butter, parsley

### Mediterranean Turkey Stuffed Peppers
- [ ] 1.25 lb prepped turkey
- [ ] 4 bell peppers, halved
- [ ] 1/2 cup cooked rice
- [ ] 1 cup marinara
- [ ] Fresh parsley

### Mediterranean Lentil & Roasted Veg Bowl
- [ ] 2 cups prepped lentils
- [ ] 1 sweet potato, cubed
- [ ] 1 zucchini, sliced
- [ ] Tahini-lemon sauce (adults)
- [ ] Olive oil, cumin, paprika

### Spinach & Feta Frittata
- [ ] 8 eggs
- [ ] 5 oz baby spinach
- [ ] 1/2 cup feta (adults' side)
- [ ] 1/4 cup milk
- [ ] Onion, garlic, olive oil

### Salmon Cakes with Cucumber-Yogurt
- [ ] 1 lb salmon (or 2 cans good-quality)
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1/4 cup almond flour
- [ ] Dill, lemon zest, scallion
- [ ] Plain Greek yogurt + cucumber + dill (sauce)

### Sous Vide Cod with Lemon-Herb Butter
- [ ] 3 cod fillets
- [ ] Butter, lemon, dill, parsley
- [ ] Asparagus, olive oil

### Mediterranean Chicken Wrap
- [ ] Sliced sous vide chicken
- [ ] Whole-wheat tortilla
- [ ] Hummus, cucumber, tomato, feta (adults)

### Greek Chicken Lunch Bowl (uses prep)
- [ ] Prepped chicken thighs
- [ ] Cucumber, tomato, hummus
- [ ] Pita (adults)

### Mediterranean Tuna Salad
- [ ] 2 cans tuna in olive oil
- [ ] Hard-boiled eggs
- [ ] Lemon, capers, parsley, olive oil
- [ ] Lettuce wraps or whole-grain bread

### Mediterranean Egg Muffins (batch)
- [ ] Spinach, sun-dried tomato, scallion
- [ ] Feta (adults' batch)
- [ ] Olive oil

### Mediterranean Power Smoothie
- [ ] 1 cup Greek yogurt
- [ ] 1 banana
- [ ] 1 cup berries
- [ ] 1 cup spinach
- [ ] 1 tbsp almond butter (adults)
- [ ] Milk to thin

### Cottage Cheese & Tomato Bowl
- [ ] Cottage cheese (low-sodium for baby)
- [ ] Cherry tomatoes, cucumber
- [ ] Olive oil, black pepper, dill

### Veggie Scramble with Feta
- [ ] 5 eggs
- [ ] Spinach, tomato, scallion
- [ ] Feta (adults)

### Greek Yogurt Bowl with Berries
- [ ] Greek yogurt (full-fat for baby, 0% or 2% adults)
- [ ] Mixed berries
- [ ] Chia seeds, honey (ADULTS only — never for baby under 12 mo)
- [ ] Almond butter (adults)
