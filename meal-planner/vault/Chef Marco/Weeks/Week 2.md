## Shopping list

### Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Apples & Fennel
- [ ] 1 pork tenderloin (~1.25 lb)
- [ ] 2 apples, wedges
- [ ] 1 fennel bulb, wedges
- [ ] Olive oil, garlic, rosemary (adults), Dijon

### Sheet Pan Greek Meatballs with Veggies
- [ ] 12 prepped meatballs
- [ ] 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- [ ] 1 zucchini, sliced
- [ ] 1 red onion, wedges
- [ ] Olive oil, oregano
- [ ] Tzatziki (adults)

### Lemony Lentil Soup
- [ ] 3 cups prepped lentils
- [ ] 1 onion, carrot, celery (mirepoix)
- [ ] 4 cups low-sodium broth
- [ ] 1 lemon
- [ ] 2 cups spinach
- [ ] Olive oil, cumin, garlic

### Italian Wedding Soup
- [ ] 6 cups low-sodium broth
- [ ] 1/2 cup acini di pepe pasta
- [ ] 3 cups baby spinach
- [ ] 1 carrot, diced
- [ ] Parmesan (adults)

### Mediterranean Lentil & Roasted Veg Bowl
- [ ] 2 cups prepped lentils
- [ ] 1 sweet potato, cubed
- [ ] Tahini-lemon sauce (adults)
- [ ] Olive oil, cumin, paprika

### Meatballs in Marinara over Polenta
- [ ] 2 cups marinara
- [ ] 1 cup polenta + 4 cups water/milk

### Zucchini Noodle Shrimp Scampi
- [ ] 1 lb shrimp
- [ ] 3 zucchini, spiralized
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves
- [ ] White wine (adults), butter, lemon, parsley

### Mediterranean Tuna Salad
- [ ] 2 cans tuna in olive oil
- [ ] Hard-boiled eggs
- [ ] Lemon, capers, parsley, olive oil
- [ ] Lettuce wraps or whole-grain bread

### Mediterranean Chicken Wrap
- [ ] Sliced sous vide chicken
- [ ] Whole-wheat tortilla
- [ ] Hummus, cucumber, tomato, feta (adults)

### Greek Chicken Lunch Bowl (uses prep)
- [ ] Prepped chicken thighs
- [ ] Cucumber, tomato, hummus
- [ ] Pita (adults)

### Veggie Scramble with Feta
- [ ] 5 eggs
- [ ] Spinach, tomato, scallion
- [ ] Feta (adults)
- [ ] Olive oil

### Mediterranean Egg Muffins (batch)
- [ ] 8 eggs
- [ ] Spinach, sun-dried tomato, scallion
- [ ] Feta (adults' batch)

### Mediterranean Power Smoothie
- [ ] 1 cup Greek yogurt
- [ ] 1 banana
- [ ] 1 cup berries
- [ ] 1 cup spinach
- [ ] 1 tbsp almond butter (adults)
- [ ] Milk to thin

### Cottage Cheese & Tomato Bowl
- [ ] Cottage cheese (low-sodium for baby)
- [ ] Cherry tomatoes, cucumber
- [ ] Olive oil, black pepper, dill

### Greek Yogurt Bowl with Berries
- [ ] Greek yogurt (full-fat for baby, 0% or 2% adults)
- [ ] Mixed berries
- [ ] Chia seeds, honey (ADULTS only — never for baby under 12 mo)
- [ ] Almond butter (adults)
